1. Construct a matrix representation of the following system of equations, and solve it using the matrix:

$$
\\begin{eqnarray}
-3x + 6y - 9z &=& 3\\\\
x - y - 2z &=& 0\\\\
5x + 5y - 7z &=& 63
\\end{eqnarray}
$$
  
Solution:
$$ x = 8, y = 6, z = 1 $$
